## Studi Kasus

### Tahap 1: PPh Pasal 21
1. Total PPh 21 setahun = **Rp34.715.000** (dari `rekap-pph21-tahunan.pdf`)

### Tahap 2: PPh Badan
1. Laba fiskal = Rp23.059.000.000 - Rp75.000.000 (bunga deposito final) = **Rp22.984.000.000**
2. PPh 31E: Rp4,8M x 11% = Rp528jt. Sisa Rp18.184.000.000 x 22% = Rp4.000.480.000. Total = **Rp4.528.480.000**
3. Kredit pajak = PPh 22 (Rp57,5jt) + PPh 23 (Rp26,8jt) = **Rp84.300.000**
4. PPh kurang bayar = Rp4.528.480.000 - Rp84.300.000 = **Rp4.444.180.000**
